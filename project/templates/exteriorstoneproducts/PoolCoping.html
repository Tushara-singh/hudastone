Pool coping is simply a term that is used to describe the cap or edge on the pool’s rim. It serves multiple purposes, not only separating your pool from the surfaces that join it but also protecting the structure of the pool itself. This edge can also help to improve safety around the pool by giving swimmers a safer, nonskid surface on which to walk.
