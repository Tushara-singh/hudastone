Product details

Color: Blue Material origin: South India

Additional name: Shahabad Limestone, Tandor Blue Limestone

Application area: Building stone, wall facades, sinks, monuments, interiors, exteriors, pool coping, sills, ornamental stone

Greyish blue in color, Tandur Blue is a beautiful limestone quarried from South India. The pale while veins on tandur blue limestone surface significantly contribute to its antique looks and for this very reason, it is quite popular among architects across the world. Impervious in nature, this tandur blue stone has a subtle texture and known for its strength.

Owing to its impressive appearance and physical properties, tandur blue limestone is regarded as a great architectural material for décor and construction projects associated with flooring applications in hallways, conservatories, kitchen, and bathrooms. Tandur Blue is also widely used for wall cladding, pavements, doorways, and patios. There are lots of tandur stone manufacturers in India that supply high-quality limestone products and articles, including tiles, pool coping stone, crazy stone, steps, and wall stones.
